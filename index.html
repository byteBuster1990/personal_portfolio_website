I consistently drive measurable impact—reducing compute costs, improving pipeline performance, and accelerating time-to-insight for business teams. What I bring to organizations is the ability to bridge business requirements with robust technical architecture: designing data platforms that are not just efficient, but strategically aligned to support analytics, AI/ML, and evolving business needs at scale.
